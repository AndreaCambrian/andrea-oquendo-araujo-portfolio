🧠 Burnout Radar
The privacy-first early warning system for human well-being — stopping the silent collapse before it starts.

Burnout costs the global economy billions — but the human cost is immeasurable. Current tools require constant monitoring through wearables or invasive therapy, creating a "Patient Adherence Gap" where people quit because it feels like work. Burnout Radar changes that.

Work placement — SteriPro Canada & HSN
May 11 – June 26, 2026 · Mississauga, ON (Weeks 1–5) & Sudbury, ON (Weeks 6–8)

Seven-week placement at SteriPro Canada — Canada's leading ISO-certified Medical Device Reprocessing provider, headquartered at 6580 Northwest Drive, Mississauga — and Health Sciences North. The first five weeks required relocating to Mississauga. Weekly meetings were held with COO Vikram, Plant Manager Afshin, and Manager Abiye throughout; expanded to include the full HSN team (Steve, James, Bryan, Roch, and Chantel) in the final weeks.

Week 1 · May 11–16
Decontamination — Mississauga HQ
H&S orientation, instrument sorting & disassembly, manual/hand wash, washer-disinfector operations, daily tests, detergent/dosing, robotics & ultrasonic cleaning. MDRAO Chapters 1–6.
Week 2 · May 18–23
Surgical Tray System (STS)
STS philosophy, recipe creation, lasering & initialization, set traceability, CRF, discrepancy reporting, inventory management, repair/replace. MDRAO Chapters 7–8.
Week 3 · May 25–30
Clean Assembly
Inspection & testing methods, assembly techniques, wrapping, workload breakdown, loaner instruments, production binder review (including endoscope procedures). MDRAO Chapters 9–10.
Week 4 · June 1–6
Sterilization
Steam & H₂O₂ cycles, load configuration, sterilization parameters, BI/CI verification, daily diagnostics, requalification procedure. MDRAO Chapter 11.
Week 5 · June 8–13
Production, Admin & AO Reprocessing
Load & product release, dispatching, case cart picking AO1, shift reports, productivity tracking, incident reports, Getinge service requests, dock-to-dock sweeps, AO full reprocessing cycle. MDRAO Chapters 12–13.
Weeks 6–7 · June 15–25
Quality Assurance — Sudbury
Passivation, SOP development, change control (3M→Steris vendor transition), incident management, HSN event log tracking, water sampling program. MDRAO Chapters 1 & 14.
Weeks 7–8 · June 11–26
HSN Hospital Program
ChronoMedic orientation (management & technician views), leadership structure, MDRD/OR/clinics, hospital & department tour, HSN CBA review. Meetings with HSN leadership team.
Outcome: permanent full-time employment offer

At the conclusion of the placement, both SteriPro Canada and Health Sciences North extended a permanent full-time employment offer — the most meaningful possible validation of placement performance in a highly regulated, quality-critical healthcare environment.
